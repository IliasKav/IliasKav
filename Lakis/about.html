Our Story

First opening in 1980, at the most central place of the beach street, Lakis' fish tavern is still standing strong serving old acquaintances and newcomers alike. Customers can experience the calmness of the sea and feel the salty breeze while enjoying our cuisine.

More than 40 years later the quality still remains intact attracting not only people from all over the world, but also loyal locals that eat at our restaurant winter and summer.
